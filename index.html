ClipFlow Publisher

ClipFlow Publisher is a personal desktop application used by its single owner (the developer) to publish his own original, locally edited short-form videos to his own social media accounts, and to read the view statistics of those posts.

What this application does

  • Uploads the owner's own finished videos to the owner's own YouTube channel (YouTube Data API), Instagram account (Instagram API), and Facebook Page (Graph API), after the owner manually approves each video.
  • Reads back the view counts and engagement statistics of those same posts so the owner can track performance.
  • Stores its settings, access tokens, and statistics only on the owner's personal computer.

What it does not do

It is not a public service: there are no third-party users, no accounts other than the owner's own, no data collection about anyone, no advertising, and nothing is sold or shared.
