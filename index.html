ClipFlow Publisher

ClipFlow Publisher is a personal desktop tool used by its single owner to publish his own edited short-form videos to his own social media accounts (TikTok, Instagram) and to read view statistics of his own posts.

It is not a public service: there are no third-party users, no accounts other than the owner's, and no data collection of any kind.
